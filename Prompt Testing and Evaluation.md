1. **Preheat the Oven**
- Preheat your oven to **375°F (190°C)**.

- In a large skillet, cook the **chopped bacon** over medium heat until crispy.
- Remove the bacon and place it on a paper towel-lined plate to drain. Set aside.

3. **Cook the Chicken**
- In the same skillet, add **1 tablespoon of olive oil** if needed.
